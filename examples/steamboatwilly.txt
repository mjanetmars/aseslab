
SPRITE SETTINGS
    Tile size:          24 by 24
    Tiles per image:    5 columns, 4 rows.
    Render all layers:  Yes

USAGE
    This voxel sprite shows how the script can render multiple layers at once,
    separating the face, ears and hat from the head, which are then combined
    on the preview.
